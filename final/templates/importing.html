Compound Interest

Compound interest is the concept of earning interest not only on the initial investment but also on the accumulated interest over time.

Diversification

Diversification is spreading your investments across different assets or asset classes to reduce risk. It helps mitigate the impact of poor performance in one investment.

Risk and Return

The principle of risk and return states that generally, higher returns are associated with higher risks. Investors need to find a balance between risk and potential rewards.
